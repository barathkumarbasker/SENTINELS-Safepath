About the Project

Safepath AI is a Predictive Safety Intelligence platform designed to enhance urban security through real-time data analysis. By integrating live incident reporting, safety zone management, and intelligent routing, the application empowers users to navigate their environment with confidence. Our mission is to leverage technology to create a safer, more transparent community experience.
